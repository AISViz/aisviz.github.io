About AISViz

AISViz, hosted at Dalhousie University, is a collaborative project dedicated to providing open-source tools like AISdb for marine data integration and analysis. Embracing the ethos of community-driven development, we empower researchers and conservationists to unlock the potential of maritime data.

Featured Tool: AISdb

AISdb stands out as a premier tool within AISViz, offering robust capabilities for integrating and analyzing Automatic Identification System (AIS) data. It supports a wide range of applications, from maritime traffic management to environmental impact assessments.
